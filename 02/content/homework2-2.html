Summary

Saekano: How to Raise a Boring Girlfriend is a Japanese light novel series by Fumiaki Maruto, with illustrations by Kurehito Misaki. It illustrates the life of members in a doujin circle targeting a AVG bestseller in ComicMarket, and how they build up friendship with each other.
